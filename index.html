Process

Flat assets, volumetric vibes

  • Use multiple layers with slight offsets and different blur radii.
  • Combine gradients for edge lighting: one soft for ambience, one sharp for rim light.
  • Apply subtle noise textures to avoid banding and give tactile feel.
  • Animate elevation on hover using transforms and brighter highlights.

Everything is pure HTML/CSS—no canvas, no 3D engine.
